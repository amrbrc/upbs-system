Terms of Service

Last Updated: July 6, 2026

Welcome to UP Bikeshare. By registering, logging into, or using our campus bicycle sharing system—including sending text messages (SMS) to our gateway or utilizing our Facebook Messenger Chatbot—you agree to be bound by these Terms of Service. Please read them carefully.

1. Eligibility and Membership

To register and use the UP Bikeshare program, you must:

  • Be an active student, faculty, or staff member of the university.
  • Possess a verified and active mobile phone number.
  • Complete the registration process and maintain a Trust Score above the suspension threshold.

2. Rules of Checkout & Operation

  • Standard Borrow Limit: The standard borrow duration limit is 6 hours per ride. You must return and lock the bicycle at a designated hub within this limit.
  • Proper Parking: Bicycles must always be parked and locked securely inside the official physical boundary coordinates of a UP Bikeshare Hub. Abandoning a bicycle outside of a hub is a breach of service.
  • Damage Reporting: If you find a bicycle broken or missing, you must report it immediately using the system interface or text command.

3. The Trust Score & Demerit System

Your membership standing is regulated by a points-based Trust System. Default rules and point modifications include:

  • Starting Score: Newly registered accounts start with 80 points.
  • Suspension Limit: If your Trust Score falls below 50 points, your account is automatically suspended. You will not be allowed to borrow any bicycles.
  • −35 Points Hit-and-Run Penalty: Deducted if a user leaves a bike broken without reporting it, and the next borrower reports the damage.
  • −5 Points False Damage Report: Deducted for reporting fake damages or false missing reports.
  • −5 Points / Hour Overtime Penalty: Deducted for every hour you exceed the 6-hour borrow limit.
  • +5 Points Bicycle Transport Reward: Awarded to users who volunteer to deliver a reported broken/stranded bike to the maintenance hub.
  • +5 Points Honesty Reward: Awarded when a clean report is confirmed by the subsequent rider.
  • +30 Points Community Service Reward: Awarded for completing a verified volunteer shift at the hub.

4. Dispute Resolution & Appeal Protocol

To protect riders from malicious reports, UP Bikeshare enforces a **Dispute Appeal Protocol**:

  1. If a subsequent rider reports your checked-out bike as broken, your account is automatically frozen.
  2. To appeal this decision, you must contact our Facebook Page (m.me/upbikeshare) and use the automated chatbot to upload a photo of the bicycle parked and locked in good condition.
  3. An administrator will review the submitted image evidence and issue a final verdict of **Innocent** (points and account restored) or **Guilty** (demerits applied).

5. Term and Termination

We reserve the right to suspend or terminate your access to the UP Bikeshare system if you violate these terms, damage equipment, or engage in behavior that harms the community or compromises system assets.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions, disputes, or feedback regarding these terms, please visit the physical UP Bikeshare Hub on campus or message our administrator team on our official Facebook Page.
