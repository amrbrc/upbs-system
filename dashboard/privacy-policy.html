UP BIKESHARE

Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 6, 2026

At UP Bikeshare, we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting the personal data of our student riders, administrators, and volunteers. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, process, and share your information in connection with the UP Bikeshare Application, including our SMS Gateway system and our Facebook Messenger Chatbot.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information to provide a secure and reliable bicycle sharing program within the university campus. The types of information we collect include:

  • Personal Identification Details: First Name, Last Name, and Student Number during registration.
  • Contact Details: Verified Mobile Number, which is used as your unique identifier to borrow and return bikes via SMS text commands.
  • Chatbot Data (Facebook Messenger): If you appeal a dispute or frozen account using our Facebook Messenger Chatbot, we collect your Facebook Page-Scoped ID (PSID) to maintain conversation state, your registered phone number to link your account, and any photographs you send to verify the bike’s condition.
  • Usage Data: Location coordinates of the bike hubs, check-out and check-in timestamps, ride durations, history logs, and trust points balance.

2. How We Use Your Information

We process your data for the following essential program operations:

  • To verify your enrollment eligibility as an active member of the university.
  • To authorize checkouts and check-ins of bicycles at physical hubs.
  • To calculate and apply your Trust Score, demerit points, and volunteer merit rewards.
  • To process dispute appeal requests and verify bicycle conditions through administrator inspection.
  • To send security codes, status alerts, borrow reminders, and notification updates via SMS and/or Messenger.

3. Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored securely using cloud databases. We implement standard security practices including SSL/TLS encryption for data in transit and AES-256 encryption at rest. Accounts, logs, and image uploads are protected against unauthorized access.

4. Data Sharing and Disclosures

UP Bikeshare is a student-led organization. We do not sell, trade, rent, or monetize your personal data with third-party advertising companies. Your data is accessed only by authorized organization administrators to moderate usage, resolve checkout conflicts, and maintain hardware assets.

5. Third-Party Integrations

Our service utilizes trusted third-party integrations to function:

  • Meta Platforms (Facebook Graph API): To manage incoming customer support chats and receive dispute appeal photos.
  • Discord API: To securely send transactional administrator alert notifications in real-time.
  • GSM Modem Gateway: To process incoming and outgoing check-out/check-in SMS alerts.

6. User Rights & Data Deletion

You have the right to request deletion of your account and historical logs. If you wish to revoke access or delete your record, please contact the UP Bikeshare administrator team or visit the physical UP Bikeshare Hub on campus. We will delete your data within 30 days of request validation.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect operational or regulatory changes. Any updates will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
