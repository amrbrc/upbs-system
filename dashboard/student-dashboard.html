Step 1 of 11

Welcome to UP Bikeshare! 🎉

Here's how UP Bikeshare works — a community bike-sharing system for UP students. Everything is operated via SMS, so you don't need internet when you're on the go.

This quick guide walks you through every step: from finding a bike to returning it and keeping your Trust Score healthy.
Step 2 of 11

Check Campus Availability

Before heading out, see how many bikes are ready across all UPBS stations on campus:

FORMAT search all
EXAMPLE search all
The system replies with a live count of available bikes at every hub (e.g. EEE: 3 bikes, VINZONS: 5 bikes).
Step 3 of 11

Check Bikes at a Hub

Found a hub with available bikes? Check which exact bicycle numbers are parked there:

FORMAT search [location]
EXAMPLE search eee

Replace [location] with the station name (e.g. palma_hall, chk, engg). You'll get a list of bike codes ready for checkout.

Step 4 of 11

Borrow a Bike via SMS

Found a bike? Text the system to start your ride:

FORMAT [bike] [from] to [to]
EXAMPLE 1 eee to chk

Replace [bike] with the bike number, [from] with pickup station, and [to] with your destination.

Step 5 of 11

Return it On Time

When you're done riding, text done to end the borrow. You have a 6-hour limit.

FORMAT done [bike]
EXAMPLE done 1
Going over 6 hours deducts −5 pts per hour of overtime. Return on time!
Step 6 of 11

Review the Bike Condition

After returning, the next rider will inspect the bike and reply to confirm its condition. When you pick up a bike, you may also be asked to reply:

GOOD CONDITION good [bike]
DAMAGED CONDITION broken [bike]
Confirming honestly earns you +1 trust point. Ignoring the prompt deducts −2 pts.
Step 7 of 11

Deliver a Broken Bike to a Hub

If a bike is broken or needs repair, you can bring it to a hub and notify the system:

FORMAT delivered [bike] [location]
EXAMPLE delivered 1 chk
This logs the bike as returned to a repair hub and helps the admin track damaged bikes quickly.
Step 8 of 11

Report a Bike

Spotted a damaged or missing bike? Report it immediately so admins can act fast:

BROKEN BIKE broken [bike]
MISSING BIKE missing [bike]
Verified honest reports earn you +5 pts. False reports deduct −5 pts.
Step 9 of 11

Other Useful Commands

Here are more SMS commands you can use anytime:

locations — List all active stations
usage [bike] — Check where a bike is parked
how — Get quick instructions via SMS
bikeshare help — Full command list via SMS
Step 10 of 11

Your Trust Score

Every student has a Trust Score. Ride responsibly to earn points — violations cost you.

+1 Honest Return
+5 Dispute Reward
−5/hr Overtime
−35 Hit & Run
Dropping below 50 points suspends your account automatically.
